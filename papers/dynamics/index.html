design paper

populace dynamics: an open, scored longitudinal layer for policy microsimulation

The paper specifies populace's longitudinal Dynamics layer — trajectory-weighted calibration, transitions as conditional models, domains of validity, and a scoring protocol under which every claim resolves, backtests, or computes exactly from statute. First validation domain: U.S. Social Security.

Read online Download PDF Design book
foundation
Builds on populace's certified cross-sectional layer — 0.038 holdout loss vs 0.317 for the enhanced CPS it replaced.
method
One weight per trajectory with entry/exit markers; a Dynamics operator mixing deterministic hazards with machine-learned earnings processes.
discipline
No 75-year point forecasts — sensitivity surfaces instead; forecast cells resolve annually; contributions merge only on held-out score.
scope
U.S. Social Security is the first validation domain; the layer is country-agnostic by construction.
