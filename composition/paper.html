Composition: what running imputation and calibration together does

The imputation and calibration dossiers each validate one operator with the other held fixed. This paper asks what happens when both run on the same file — measured where neither single-operator study can look: realized landmines, calibration's power to repair a bad imputation, the target-vs-survey trade-off as an unscalarized Pareto frontier, and whether effective support quietly collapses under composition.
